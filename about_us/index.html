About Us

I sale 10a mink hair, wigs, colored wigs/bundles and custom wigs. All my hair is from one donor. This hair can last for years. Mink hair is the hottest hair out right now and I have it! All the different styles of hair is inside my logo, I also have a number of textures of hair such as Brazilian, Indian, Cambodian, Malaysian, etc. wigs can only be customized if the hair is bought from me. All hair is shipped out in 2-4 business days.

Return policy: No refund after color or cutting on the hair!

How to take care of the hair: Use wide tooth comb if using a comb, but I prefer a wide tooth brush(wig brush). Start from the bottom and go up, be gentle. Wash the hair as if it is your hair, 2 to 3 times a week, preferably. Doing a deep conditioning on the hair once a week will keep the hair hydrated as if it was coming from your scalp. Let conditioner sit in for at lease 15 mins, then wash out and let it air dry or use blow dryer on cool. If the ends start to have split ends cut with salon scissors every 6-10 weeks.

You shouldn't comb: Deep wave and kinky curly hair.

Wigs: Wigs are made to look natural, if you want 3 bundles and a frontal as a wig you will have to buy 3 bundles, a closure/frontal and a wig customize service fee.

Bundles: You have to maintain longer bundles more than shorter bundles. Be gentle with bundles.
